Step 1: Define Your Goal

Before writing, ask yourself: What do I want visitors to do? Follow me? Buy a product? Click a link?

Step 2: Use Keywords

Instagram Search is becoming more powerful. If you are a fitness coach, include "Fitness Coach" in your name field or bio text so people can find you.
